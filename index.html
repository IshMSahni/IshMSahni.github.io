ABOUT ME

I am currently a second year undergraduate student at Berklee College of Music dual majoring in Music Production and Engineering & Music Business Management.

My mission is to acquire and develop core skills required to not only succeed in the industry to excel at what I do come 2021.

With a focus on fostering a sense of community among artists in the industry and revolutionizing aspects of the performing arts, my experiences, projects, and involvement within the community are at the core of my vision and mission here at Berklee.

I am currently involved with multiple organizations within the USA, Canada, UAE and Spain. Feel free to brows through my portfolio for more specifics.
